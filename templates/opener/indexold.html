The AI-Proctor: A Necessary Ingredient for Credible Online Certificates!

The AI-Proctor system ensures the credibility of the exam results, which is important to keep the value of courses’ certificates. It ensures that any dishonest behavior by any student during the exam is reported. In case of detection of suspicious activities, a real time report is sent to the instructor in a very intuitive dashboard that shows what’s happening on the student’s site. At the end of the exam, detailed reports for the suspicious activities and cheating attempts of every student is sent to the instructor, in addition to a summary report for the whole class.
The AI-Proctor system is empowered by Artificial Intelligent algorithms to make the proctoring process easier than ever. The system gives the instructor an unprecedented proctoring experience and gives the student a super easy interface to start and end the exam.

Real-Time Monitoring

During the exam, the instructor will be notified with any suspicious activity from the exam takers.
The instructor then can after confirming that this suspicious activity is not permitted, he/she can send a notification to the exam taker, or remove him/her from the exam permanently.

Tabs monitoring

While taking the exam if the student opened any other tab the AI Proctor will notify the instructor in real-time with the number of tabs currently opened, their URLs and a screen-shot. When the exam end the instructor can download the full detailed detections of the opened tabs, URLs, the time they were opened in and a video of that detected action.

Foreground Program

If the exam taker opens or alt-tab already opened program over the Chrome browser the instructor will be notified immediately with the Program in the foreground and a screen-shot. When the exam end the instructor can download the full detailed detections of the opened Programs, the time they were opened in and a video of that detected action.

Stream

The instructor can open any online student's camera and share his/her screen with low frame rate so it will not affect the internet connection for either the instructor or the exam taker.

Works even with Internet disconnections

If the Internet is disconnected at a certain student's place, the system caches all cheating events and sends them to the instructor once the connection is restored. Cheating events are encrypted and stored until Internet is retrieved. Advanced techniques are used to verify no fraud activities are done by offline students.

Exam URL Safe

The exam page is opened automatically through the AI Proctoring system in incognito mode and in full screen. if the exam taker try to change from full screen to see the exam URL it will re-change the mode to full screen.

Face verification

The AI-Proctor opens the exam only after facial verification. The student uploads his picture during registration, and it is used in all his exams to make sure he doesn't leave the examination place.

Presence Detection

If there was another person near the exam taker (in the cam range), the instructor will be notified. if the exam taker leave from the cam range of view the instructor will be notified.
